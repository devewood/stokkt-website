What Stokkt does

From scan to dinner.
In under a minute.

Stokkt brings your pantry, meal plan, and shopping list together in one place — so less food gets wasted and every meal feels effortless.

🧾

Scan & stock instantly

Photograph a receipt and your entire shop lands in your pantry — quantities, categories, and expiry dates captured automatically. No manual entry required.

📅

Plan your week in one tap

Generate a full week of meal ideas built around your pantry, your household, and your dietary preferences. Stokkt prioritises items expiring soon so nothing goes to waste.

👨‍🍳

Cook Mode — hands free

Open any recipe and Cook Mode guides you through every step. Your screen stays awake, you tap through each stage, and when you finish your pantry updates automatically. No more phone-propping, no more losing your place.

🍳

300,000+ real recipes

Match your pantry to tried-and-tested recipes you can cook right now — or let AI generate something completely original from exactly what you have in stock.

🛒

Your list, built for you

Stokkt knows what's running low, what's expired, and what your meal plan needs. Your shopping list writes itself — organised and ready to go.

♻️

Zero food waste

Push notifications alert you 3 days before anything expires. Expiring items are automatically prioritised in recipe suggestions so they get used, not thrown out.
